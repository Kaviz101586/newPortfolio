New Jersey Trail

The New Jersey Trail is a throwback to an old classic, with a "Joisey" spin! Embark on your adventure across our beautiful state as you rush to South Jersey in an effort to make your friend's wedding. You've got food, water, and gas - what trouble could you possibly have? As any New Jerseyan that's traveled the highways knows - plenty! Shore traffic, gang trouble, flying pork rolls - anything can happen!!

Happy Healthy Tummies

Happy Healthy Tummies is an appllication built to find restaurants based on location, food choice, and nutrition information unique to each user. Our goal was to build an app accessible for health conscience people - those who lead busy lives and may not have time always to make a meal. Using each user's specifications around nutritional details - HHT will pinpoint nearby locations for you to order from - and then map it for you so you can head straight there!
